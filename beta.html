Welcome to
Beta Testing

This Beta Test is a simplified version of what the full Corder platform will offer. We're currently running it on a small scale with a curated list of fresh products and a manual checkout process.

As a beta tester, you'll help us understand if students are willing to commit to orders based on group pricing, test our pricing structure, and see if we can successfully coordinate small group orders.

What's Different?

Here's how our beta version compares to the full Corder platform:

Feature Full Version Beta Version
Product Variety Dozens of local goods from verified producers 3–5 curated fresh produce items
Group Order System Live progress tracking + auto-charging if group fills Manual tracking via Discord and Google Sheets
Payment Card authorization, automatic charge or refund Manual e-transfer, refund/credit after group fills
Delivery Smart routing & multiple options Fixed pickup time/location or simple delivery

How Beta Works

Our beta testing process is designed to be simple and rewarding. Here's what you need to know about participating in our community-driven food platform.

Click on any section below to learn more about the specific mechanisms and rules that make our beta testing program effective.

1. View Products & Prices

+
-

On this page, you'll see a list of fresh products with:

  • Solo Price (e.g., $9)
  • Group Price (e.g., $6 if 5+ join)

2. Join the Order

+
-

Fill out the short order form with:

  • Name
  • Email
  • Discord Username
  • Quantity
  • Pickup Time Preference

3. Submit Payment

+
-

To confirm your spot, send an e-transfer to:

  • Email: justinyu070203@gmail.com
  • Amount: [Displayed on form]
  • Message: "Corder Beta + Your Full Name"

You will be added to our Discord server, where we post live updates on group order status.

4. What Happens Next

+
-

If the group fills by deadline (e.g., 5+ join by Wednesday 11:59 PM), you will receive a refund or credit for the group discount amount.

If the group doesn't fill, you can choose to:

  • Proceed at the solo price (no refund)
  • Cancel and receive a full refund

We will notify all participants via Discord and email before Thursday 12PM.

Payment Process

+
-

In the full Corder app:

  • You'd link your card and only be charged if the group fills

In this beta version:

  • We collect full payment up front via e-transfer
  • If the group fills, we manually refund the difference or credit it to your next order
  • This helps us keep things simple while still simulating real behavior

Beta Policies

+
-
  • Order Limit: Only 100 orders are accepted in this beta cycle
  • Cutoff Time: Orders must be placed and paid by Wednesday 11:59 PM
  • Pickup: Orders will be available for pickup on Thursday/Friday at [Campus Location]
  • Refunds: If group discount applies, refund/credit will be processed by Sunday evening
  • No Resale or Abuse: Please do not submit multiple orders under fake names — we verify via Discord and e-transfer matching
